About

The
Story

I didn't start behind a camera. I started behind a timeline cutting a national TV series frame by frame, learning that every edit is a decision and every decision tells a story.

Five years later, I've taught filmmaking including cinematography, lighting, sound, and video editing served as Creative Director for national and international brands, and won Best Editing & Sound Design at the Tom Film Awards for Tinsae a film that proved what I already believed: great editing isn't invisible, it's unforgettable.

Today I work at the intersection of cinematic storytelling and AI enhanced production helping brands create content that doesn't just look good, it lands.

Currently serving as AI and Innovation Creative Director at a creative agency in Addis Ababa leading AI tool adoption and next-generation content production.
